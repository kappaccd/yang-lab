Welcome to Our Lab

Our research focuses on the design and development of novel coordination polymers and MOFs with magnetic, catalytic, and sensing functionalities. We aim to explore structure-property relationships through crystal engineering and materials chemistry approaches.

Research Interests

Molecule-based magnets
Design and development of novel coordination polymers
Functional metal-organic frameworks (MOFs)
